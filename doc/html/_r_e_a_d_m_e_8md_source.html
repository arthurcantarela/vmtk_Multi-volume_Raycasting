66 # Instructions
67 
68 w: rotate the volume around x axis (clockwise)
69 s: rotate the volume around x axis (counter-clockwise)
70 a: rotate the volume around z axis (clockwise)
71 d: rotate the volume around z axis (counter-clockwise)
72 q: rotate the volume around y axis (clockwise)
73 e: rotate the volume around y axis (counter-clockwise)
74 r: reset the transformation
